How It Works

A clear, safe, step-by-step process for both families and sports professionals.

👨‍👩‍👧

For Parents & Guardians

1
Create a free parent account
Register with your email and cellphone. We verify both with a one-time PIN to confirm your identity.
2
Give your consent
We'll explain clearly — in plain language, not legal jargon — exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and what your rights are under POPIA.
3
Build your child's profile
Upload their sport, province, school type, achievements, a photo, and a short video link (YouTube or Google Drive). A step-by-step wizard guides you through.
4
We review every profile
Our admin team reviews the profile within 48 hours before any scout can see it. We check the content is appropriate and complete.
5
You stay in control — always
Turn visibility on or off at any time. You see who views the profile. You approve every contact request before a scout gets your details. You can delete everything at any time.
🏫

For Scouts, Schools & Coaches

1
Apply for verified access
Submit your application with your organisation details, role, and reason for access. We may ask for ID and proof of employment or federation affiliation.
2
We verify you
A human reviews your application within 5 business days. We check your identity and organisation. If approved, you receive a welcome email with your login link.
3
Search and discover
Filter athletes by sport, province, age group, position, and achievement level. All activity is logged for child protection purposes.
4
Request contact — through the parent
Send a message through the platform. It goes directly to the parent or guardian — never to the child. The parent decides whether to share their contact details with you.
5
Connect with the family
If the parent accepts, they share their contact details with you. Your conversation continues outside the platform, but all platform activity remains on record.

Privacy Notice

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: July 2026 | Applies to scoutrepublic.online

1. Who we are

Scout Republic is a South African youth sports talent discovery platform operated by Johann Moller (johannzackmoller@gmail.com). We are the Responsible Party for your personal information as defined by the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

2. What information we collect

Parent / Guardian information
Full name, email address, cellphone number, province, relationship to child, and how you heard about us.
Child / Athlete information
First name, date of birth, primary sport, and school name (optional). We do not collect your child's surname, ID number, or home address at registration.
Scout / Coach / School information
Full name, email address, cellphone number, organisation name, role, and a written statement of purpose.

3. Why we collect your information

  • To create and manage your child's athlete profile on the platform.
  • To allow verified scouts, coaches, and academies to discover your child's sporting talent.
  • To send you notifications when a scout views your child's profile.
  • To communicate with you about your registration, platform updates, and relevant opportunities.
  • To verify the identity and affiliation of scout and school applicants before granting access.
  • To comply with our legal obligations under POPIA and other applicable South African law.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal information on the basis of your explicit consent, given when you tick the consent checkboxes on our registration form. You may withdraw your consent at any time by emailing johannzackmoller@gmail.com. Withdrawing consent will result in your information and your child's profile being removed from our platform.

5. Who can see your child's information

Important: scouts never see your personal contact details.
Verified scouts can view your child's first name, sport, and general province only. They cannot see your email, phone number, your child's surname, date of birth, school name, or home address.

All contact between scouts and families happens through Scout Republic only. A scout may send a contact request through the platform — we will forward this to you, the parent, so you can decide whether to respond. Direct communication between scouts and children is prohibited and grounds for immediate removal.

6. How long we keep your information

We retain your information and your child's profile for as long as your account is active, or until you request deletion. You may request deletion at any time by emailing johannzackmoller@gmail.com. We will delete all your information within 14 business days of receiving your request.

7. Where your information is stored

Your personal information is stored securely on servers in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany) via Supabase. This location was chosen to ensure a high standard of data protection consistent with POPIA requirements.

8. Your rights under POPIA

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may ask us to correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You may ask us to delete your information and your child's profile at any time.
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Complaints: You may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa at inforeg.org.za.

To exercise any of these rights, email johannzackmoller@gmail.com.

9. Information Officer

The Information Officer responsible for Scout Republic's compliance with POPIA is Johann Moller, reachable at johannzackmoller@gmail.com.

10. Changes to this notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email.

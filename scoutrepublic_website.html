How It Works

A clear, safe, step-by-step process for both families and sports professionals.

👨‍👩‍👧

For Parents & Guardians

1
Create a free parent account
Register with your email and cellphone. We verify both with a one-time PIN to confirm your identity.
2
Give your consent
We'll explain clearly — in plain language, not legal jargon — exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and what your rights are under POPIA.
3
Build your child's profile
Upload their sport, province, school type, achievements, a photo, and a short video link (YouTube or Google Drive). A step-by-step wizard guides you through.
4
We review every profile
Our admin team reviews the profile within 48 hours before any scout can see it. We check the content is appropriate and complete.
5
You stay in control — always
Turn visibility on or off at any time. You see who views the profile. You approve every contact request before a scout gets your details. You can delete everything at any time.
🏫

For Scouts, Schools & Coaches

1
Apply for verified access
Submit your application with your organisation details, role, and reason for access. We may ask for ID and proof of employment or federation affiliation.
2
We verify you
A human reviews your application within 5 business days. We check your identity and organisation. If approved, you receive a welcome email with your login link.
3
Search and discover
Filter athletes by sport, province, age group, position, and achievement level. All activity is logged for child protection purposes.
4
Request contact — through the parent
Send a message through the platform. It goes directly to the parent or guardian — never to the child. The parent decides whether to share their contact details with you.
5
Connect with the family
If the parent accepts, they share their contact details with you. Your conversation continues outside the platform, but all platform activity remains on record.

Safety & Privacy

Child safety is not a feature.
It is the foundation of everything we do.

Our 8 Safety Promises

🔒

No public profiles

No athlete profile is ever visible without a logged-in, verified account. Your child cannot be found by searching Google.

✅

Consent before everything

No child's data is stored without the explicit, documented consent of their parent or guardian. Consent is versioned and logged.

🧑‍💼

Every scout is vetted by a human

We individually review and verify every scout, coach, and school representative before granting access.

📋

Full audit trail

Every profile view is logged. We know who saw what, when, and from which account. Parents can see this log.

📵

No direct child contact

Scouts can only contact parents or guardians. A child's contact details are never shared with any scout.

🇿🇦

POPIA compliant

We follow South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act in all data handling. Your data is never sold or shared.

🗑️

Right to erasure

Parents can request full deletion of their child's data at any time. We complete deletion within 30 days.

🚨

Abuse reporting

Any user can report a concern at any time. Reports are reviewed by a human within 24 hours. Action is taken immediately.

How We Handle Your Data (POPIA)

▸
What we collect: Your name, email, phone. Your child's name, DOB, sport, school, photos, videos, achievements.
▸
Why we collect it: So vetted scouts can discover your child's talent and contact you about opportunities.
▸
Who we share it with: Only with individually verified scouts and schools. Never with the public, never sold, never advertised.
▸
How long we keep it: While your profile is active, plus 12 months after you close your account. Delete any time.
▸
Your rights: Access, correct, delete, or stop processing your data. Email privacy@scoutrepublic.co.za.
▸
To complain: Contact the Information Regulator of South Africa at inforeg.org.za.
🚨
